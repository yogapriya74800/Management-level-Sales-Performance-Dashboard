# Business Analytics Task 2 – Advanced KPI Analysis & Interactive Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales performance data and creating an interactive business dashboard using **Microsoft Excel** and **Power BI**. The objective is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights through Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), visual analytics, and business recommendations.

The analysis was performed on a dataset containing **200 sales orders from FY 2024**, covering multiple regions, product categories, and monthly sales trends. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify growth opportunities, and support data-driven decision-making. 



 Project Objectives

* Analyze sales, profit, and order performance.
* Calculate and track key business KPIs.
* Identify revenue and profitability trends.
* Compare regional and product-category performance.
* Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard for business monitoring.
* Generate actionable business recommendations based on data insights.

 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The following KPIs were calculated and visualized:

| KPI                 | Description                             |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Total Sales         | Total revenue generated from all orders |
| Total Profit        | Net profit earned from sales            |
| Profit Margin       | Profit as a percentage of sales         |
| Total Orders        | Number of sales transactions            |
| Total Quantity      | Total units sold                        |
| Average Order Value | Average revenue per order               |
 



 Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard includes:

* KPI Cards
* Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
* Profit by Region Comparison
* Top 10 Products Performance Table
* Category-wise Sales Distribution
* Sales vs Profit Scatter Analysis
* Interactive Filtering and Drill-down Features 



 Key Business Insights

 1. Revenue vs Profit Imbalance

Electronics generated the highest sales but showed relatively low profit margins, reducing overall profitability. 

 2. Regional Performance Disparity

A region with lower order volume delivered the highest profit per order, indicating a high-value customer segment. 

 3. Seasonal Sales Peaks

Sales peaked during the fourth quarter (October–December), with November recording the highest revenue. 

 4. Office Supplies Analysis

Office Supplies generated the highest order volume but the lowest average revenue per transaction. 

 5. High-Sales, Low-Margin Products

Several high-revenue products delivered low profit margins, requiring pricing and cost optimization. 

 6. Furniture Category Opportunity

Furniture products generated high revenue despite lower sales volume, making them valuable for premium sales strategies. 


 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following actions were recommended:

1. Margin Recovery Program

   * Review products with margins below 15%.
   * Renegotiate supplier contracts.
   * Establish minimum margin thresholds.

2. Q4 Revenue Maximization

   * Increase inventory before peak season.
   * Launch targeted marketing campaigns.

3. Regional Key Account Strategy

   * Focus on high-profit customers.
   * Implement loyalty programs.

4. Product Bundling

   * Bundle Office Supply products to increase average order value.

5. Monthly Dashboard Reviews

   * Monitor KPI performance regularly.
   * Take corrective actions based on dashboard insights.
